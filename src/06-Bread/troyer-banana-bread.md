# Banana Bread

## Overview

- Yield: 1 bread loaf
- Prep Time: 15 mins
- Cook Time: 1 hr
- Total Time: 1 hr 15 mins

## Ingredients

- 1/2 Cup shortening
- 1 Cup sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 Cups Flour
- 1 Tbsp milk
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 pinch of salt
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed (more ripe == better)

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
---
2. Grease pan.
---
3. Cream shortening and sugar, add beaten eggs.
---
4. Mix in the milk.
---
5. Mix in the baking soda.
---
6. Mix in the salt.
---
7. Mix in the flour.
---
8. Mix in the bananas.
---
9. Bake at 350 deg F for 1 hr.
---
