# Mozzarella Stuffed Meatballs

## Ingredients

- 1 pound hot Italian sausage

- 1/2 tsp garlic powder

- 2 tsp salt

- 1 tsp black pepper

- 1 cup bread crumbs

- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

- 2 eggs

- 1/2 cup whole milk

- 1/2 cup chopped parsley
