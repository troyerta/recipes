# Easy Bourbon Eggnog

## Overview

- Serves: 6 small glasses

- Prep Time: 5 mins

- Total Time: 5 mins

## Ingredients

- 1 litre Eggnog Regular or Light will work

- 6 oz Bourbon

## Method


1. Mix eggnog and bourbon together in a large pitcher. Add ice if desired.
---
2. Top with a pinch of nutmeg and/or cinnamon if desired.
