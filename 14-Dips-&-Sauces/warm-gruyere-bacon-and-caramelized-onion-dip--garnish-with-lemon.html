Warm Gruyere Bacon and Caramelized Onion Dip- Garnish with Lemon

Overview

  • Yield: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil

  • 3 1/2 cups chopped onion

  • 1 teaspoon sugar

  • 2 ounces Gruyère cheese, shredded plus additional for sprinkling on top

  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives, divided

  • 1/3 cup canola mayonnaise

  • 1/3 cup light sour cream

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • 3 bacon slices, cooked and crumbled

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  1. Swirl the olive oil in the pan and add the onions. Cook on low for 20 minutes, sprinkle onions with sugar and cook on low for an additional 45 minutes to one hour. Stir occasionally so they don’t burn.
  1. In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, sour cream, bacon, 1 tablespoon chives, salt and pepper until well mixed. Stir in shredded Gruyere and caramelized onions. Place in one quart baking dish, sprinkle 2 tablespoons Gruyere on top and bake for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
  1. Sprinkle with remaining chives and serve warm with crostini or crackers.

Notes

  • If you are using frozen caramelized onions, simply thaw and add to the rest of the ingredients before baking.
