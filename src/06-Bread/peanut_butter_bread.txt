Peanut Butter Bread

Adapted from https://old.reddit.com/r/Old_Recipes/comments/cqwuq1/i_made_peanut_butter_bread_from_the_1932_five/ex08q5g/

Is starred.

INGREDIENTS

2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/3 cups milk
1/2+ cups peanut butter

STEPS

1) Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

2) Mix together dry ingredients.

3) Mix in the milk, then the peanut butter.

4) Scrape into greased loaf pan and bake for about 1 hour.
