# The Frenchy

## Overview

- Yield: 1

- Prep Time: 5 mins

- Total Time: 5 mins

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz Pear Vodka

- 3 oz Pineapple Juice

- 1 oz Cranberry Juice

## Method

1. In a chilled rocks glass filled with ice, add your vodka and pineapple juice and stir. Top with a splash of cranberry juice.
