# Raspberry Fizz

## Overview

- Yield: 4

## Ingredients

- 8 oz Raspberry Syrup

- 4 oz Red Grapefruit juice

- 4 cups club soda chilled

- 2 cups crushed ice

## Method

1. In a pitcher add raspberry syrup, grapefruit juice, club soda and mix until raspberry syrup is diluted.
---

2. Add crushed ice and serve.
---
