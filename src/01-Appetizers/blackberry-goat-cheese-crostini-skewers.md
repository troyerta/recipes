# Blackberry Goat Cheese Crostini Skewers

## Overview

- Yield: 25 skewers
- Prep Time: 10 mins
- Total Time: 10 mins

## Ingredients

- 25 mini fresh mozzarella balls

- 25 blackberries

- 25 basil leaves

- 25 cucumber chunks

- Balsamic glaze

- Bamboo skewers

## Method

1. Using 3â€³ bamboo skewers or similar, layer your ingredients starting with the mozzarella balls, then a folded basil leaf followed by a blackberry and a chunk of cucumber.
---
2. Line a tray with your finished skewers and refrigerate or serve right away.
---
3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze right before serving.
