Boozy Indian Chai

Adapted from https://old.reddit.com/r/GifRecipes/comments/fz3m5e/boozy_tea/fn316fv/

Is starred.

Servings: 1

INGREDIENTS

5 green cardamom pods
3 black peppercorns
1 inch cinnamon stick
1 pinch of clove powder
1 tsp crushed ginger
2 tsp tea leaves
400 ml water
4 Tbsp condensed milk
30 mL dark rum

STEPS

1) Crush Cardamom pods, cinnamon, ginger and cloves, keep aside.

2) In a saucepan, Add tea leaves to 13 oz water and let it brew.

3) Add ginger and spices, and let it

4) Simmer for 1 min

5) Add 4 tsp of condensed milk.

6) Add 1 oz (30 ml) of Rum of your choice.

7) Simmer for 1 min
